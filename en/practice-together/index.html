Practice Together

Answers!

Practice math together in the classroom with our interactive exercise! Perfect for practicing multiplication tables, addition, subtraction, and division in an engaging way. Teachers can easily adapt the exercise to the class's needs by selecting specific tables and operations.

The exercise displays numbers in large format on the screen, making it perfect for whole-class practice. Choose between practicing a set number of problems or run in unlimited mode for continuous practice. Adjust the display time according to student levels and choose whether to show answers after the exercise.

This tool is particularly effective for:

  • Strengthening students' multiplication table skills
  • Practicing mental arithmetic in addition and subtraction
  • Developing understanding of division
  • Creating a fun and interactive learning environment where everyone can participate

A perfect complement to math education that makes it fun to practice together!

Structure and levels

Pick an operation and range, set display time and whether to show answers. Run 2–4 short rounds, pausing for whole‑class discussion. Adjust pace to the group and alternate between a single table and mixed problems.

Teaching tips

  • Model a few examples, then have everyone respond together.
  • Make strategies explicit: number bonds, bridging through ten, fact families.
  • End with reflection: What helped? What needs more practice?
