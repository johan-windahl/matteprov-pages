Practice addition the fun way

This addition game builds speed and accuracy in mental math. Play on a level that feels challenging but fair, then raise the difficulty as you improve. Use practice mode to focus on thinking before chasing a high score.

Adjust for grade level

  • Kindergarten and grade 1: set the highest number to 10 and play in practice mode.
  • Grades 2-3: highest number 20-50, focus on bridging through ten.
  • Grades 4-6: highest number 100 and a higher difficulty level.

Teaching tips

  • Warm up for 2-3 minutes on a lower level to build speed.
  • Focus on number bonds and bridging through ten for faster answers.
  • Vary the highest number so the tasks match your goals.
